## Matrix

* matrix

$$
\begin{matrix}
a & b \\
c & d \\
\end{matrix}
$$


* bmatrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

* pmatrix

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

* vmatrix

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 \\
\end{vmatrix}
$$

* determinate

$$
\det{\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 \\
\end{vmatrix}}
\\
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
